The 95.9% Rule — When Does Overall Hit 5-Stars?

Each cell = % of reviews giving Overall=5 when Food (rows) and Service (columns) have those values. Hover any cell for review count and interpretation. The pattern is unmistakable.

When Food=5 AND Service=5 (43.4% of all reviews), Overall is also 5-star in 95.9% of cases. Drop either to 4 and the probability falls to ~73%. Drop to 3 and it collapses below 20%.
